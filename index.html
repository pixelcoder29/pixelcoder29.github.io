Frequently Asked Questions

Will my pets be safe during service? +

Your pets' safety is our top priority. We sanitize our tools and boots between properties, secure all gates, and handle every yard with care.

How do you ensure your tools and equipment are properly sanitized? +

We use kennel-grade disinfectant/sanitizer that's safe for pets to thoroughly disinfect all our tools and boots between properties. This prevents the transmission of diseases like parvo and other dog-related illnesses, keeping all pets in our service area safe.

Can you clean if my dog is in the yard? +

Yes, we love pets! For safety, dogs that are uncomfortable with strangers should be kept inside during service. Otherwise, our team is happy to work around your furry friends.

Do I need to be home? +

No, you don't have to be home. We just need access to your yard. You'll receive a text when we're on our way so you always know when to expect us.

What if I need to skip a scheduled service? +

Life happens! Just notify us in advance and we'll take care of the reschedule. No service, no charge.

Do you work in bad weather? +

We service rain or shine, except when driving conditions are unsafe (icy roads or extreme weather). Safety for both you and our team is always our top concern.

Are there any hidden fees or extra charges for the initial cleanup? +

No, our quoted price includes the initial cleanup and all ongoing services. There are no hidden fees or additional charges.

Do I have to handle the pet waste myself? +

Never! We haul away and properly dispose of all pet waste at no extra cost, leaving your yard clean, hassle-free, and ready for your pets to enjoy.

How do you handle payment? +

We charge your card on file the day before each scheduled service, and you only pay for services that are completed.

How often should I schedule service? +

We offer weekly or bi-weekly service plans to keep your yard clean, safe, and odor-free. Choose the frequency that fits your needs.

Where do you provide service? +

We proudly serve Portland Oregon, with comprehensive coverage throughout East Portland from the Willamette River to the eastern city limits.

What if I'm not satisfied or there's an issue? +

Your satisfaction is guaranteed. If anything isn't right, contact us immediately, and we'll resolve it promptly.
